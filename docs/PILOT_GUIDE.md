# District #10 Pilot Implementation Guide

## Pilot Overview

- **Duration:** 6 months (January - June 2026)
- **Location:** District #10, Montserrado County
- **Target:** 75 communities, 75 leaders
- **Goal:** Test and refine CLISPConnect before national rollout

## Phase 1: Community Mapping (Month 1-2)

### Week 1-2: Preparation
- Train registry officers
- Prepare mapping materials
- Coordinate with district officials

### Week 3-8: Field Work
- Visit all 75 communities
- Collect GPS coordinates
- Document community boundaries
- Register in CLISPConnect

## Phase 2: Leadership Registration (Month 2-3)

### Week 9-12: Documentation
- Document leadership structures
- Verify leader identities
- Upload to registry
- Issue recognition letters

## Phase 3: Training (Month 3-4)

### Week 13-16: Capacity Building
- Conduct leadership training
- Train on digital reporting
- Distribute mobile devices
- Practice reporting

## Phase 4: Reporting (Month 4-6)

### Week 17-24: Weekly Reports
- Leaders submit weekly reports
- Monitor compliance
- Address issues
- Generate monthly summaries

## Success Metrics

| Metric | Target | Measurement |
